# Metrics & Trends

Analyze your team's performance over time with visual metrics and trend charts.

## Trend Charts

The **Trend Charts** tool allows you to visualize volume and velocity trends directly in your spreadsheet.

1. Navigate to the **Metrics** tab (line chart icon 📈).
2. Select your **Trend Period**:
   - **Daily**: Shows the last 30 days.
   - **Weekly**: Shows the last 12 weeks.
   - **Monthly**: Shows the last 12 months.
3. Click **Generate Trend Chart**.

A new sheet named **"Jira Trend"** will be created with a visualization of your issue creation vs. resolution volume.

### Understanding the Metrics
- **Created**: The number of new issues identified in the period.
- **Resolved**: The number of issues moved to a "Done" statusCategory.
- **Backlog Health**: A visual representation of whether your team is keeping up with incoming work.
