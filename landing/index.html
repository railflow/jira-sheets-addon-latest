Frequently Asked Questions

Is my Jira data secure?

Absolutely. We use secure OAuth authentication and never store your API tokens on our servers. All data transfers are encrypted.

Can I sync multiple Jira projects?

Yes! Use JQL queries to pull issues from any project or combination of projects. Each sheet can have its own query.

Does it work with Jira Cloud and Server?

Currently we support Jira Cloud. Server/Data Center support is on our roadmap.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, no hidden fees.
